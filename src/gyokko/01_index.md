# Gyokko ryū kosshijutsu 玉虎流骨指術

The three levels also equate to Ten Chi Jin as:

## Ten ryaku 天略 – heaven strategy
Uchū gasshō 宇宙合掌 – universe prayer

## Chi ryaku 地略 – earth strategy
Futen goshin gasshō 風天護身合掌 – wind-heaven self protection prayer

## Jin ryaku 人略 – man strategy
Chi sui ka fu henka no kamae hannō banetsu gasshō 地水火風変化之構反応汎溢合掌 – earth-water-fire-wind changing posture response of the flood prayer
