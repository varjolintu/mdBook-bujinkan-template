# Gohō no kamae 五法之構 - five postures

## Hira no kamae 平之構 - flat posture
## Hira ichimonji no kamae 平一文字之構 - flat line posture
## Seigan no kamae 青眼之構 - clear sight posture, aiming at the eyes
## Katate hichō no kamae 片手飛鳥之構 - single hand flying bird posture
## Kōsei no Kamae 攻勢之構 - aggresive posture
