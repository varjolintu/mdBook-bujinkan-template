# Jutte 十手, Tessen 鉄扇, Kodachi 小太刀

## Juttejutsu 十手術, Tessenjutsu 鉄扇術

### Kamae 構

#### Musō no kamae 無想之構 – clear mind posture
#### Suichō no kamae 水鳥之構 – water bird posture
#### Ichi no kamae 一之構 – first posture
#### Seigan no kamae 青眼之構 – natural eye posture
#### Ten no kamae 天之構 – heaven posture

### Kata 型

#### Kiri no Hito Ha 桐之一葉 – falling paulowina leaf – arrival of autumn
#### Rakka 落花 – falling blossoms
#### Gorin Kudaki 五輪碎 – five rings crush
#### Mizu Tori 水鳥 – water bird
#### Mawari Dori 廻捕 – spinning capture

## Kodachijutsu 小太刀術

### Kamae 構

#### Seigan no kamae 青眼之構 – aiming at the eyes posture
#### Naka seigan no kamae 中青眼之構 – middle aiming at the eyes posture
#### Otonashi no kamae 音無し之構 – soundless posture

### Kata 型

#### Hichō no ken 飛鳥の剣 – flying bird sword
#### Shishi geki 獅子撃 – guardian lion attack
#### Jūji ken 十字剣 – cross sword
