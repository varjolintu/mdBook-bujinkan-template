# Naginata 薙刀, Bisentō 眉尖刀

## Binaginatajutsu 秘薙刀術 – secret naginata art

### Kamae 構

#### Hassō no kamae 八相之構 – eight apparitions position
#### Seigan no kamae 青眼之構 – aiming at the eyes position
#### Yoko ichimonji no kamae 横一文字之構 – side one line position
#### Hira ichimonji no kamae 平一文字之構 – flat one line position
### Naka seigan no kamae 中青眼之構 – middle aiming at the eyes position

### Kata 型

#### Nagi taoshi 薙倒 – mowing knockdown
#### Sukui age 掬上 – scoop up
#### Battō 拔倒 – Hataki Taoshi – extract and knock down
#### Ashi barai 足拂 – sweep out the legs
#### Hattō 撥倒 – Hane taoshi – brush up and fell
#### Kuri dashi 繰出 – turning lunge
#### Zengo nagi 前後薙 – mow down to front and rear
#### Sashi chigai 差違 – exchanging thrusts
#### Tobi kiri 飛切 – flying or jumping cut


## Bisentōjutsu 眉尖刀術 – literally ‘sharp eyebrow’ sword

### Kata 型

#### Ōshin 汪振 – deep swing
#### Kakugyaku 鬲逆 – tripod reverse
#### Chikusha 竹斜 – bamboo slant, cutting bnamboo
#### Namiba 波刃 – wave edge
#### Battō 抜刀 – extracting sword
#### Gisen 曦先 – preceding the sun, before the dawn
#### Shintō 伸刀 – stretching sword
#### Seitō 惺刀 – realization sword
#### Miken 魅剣 – bewitching blade
