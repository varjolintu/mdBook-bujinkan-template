# Bikenjutsu 秘剣術 - secret sword art

## 構 Kamae
### Ichi no kamae 一之構 - one/first
### Daijōdan no kamae 大上段之構 - big upper level
### Chūdan no kamae 中段之構 - middle level
### Gedan no kamae 下段之構 - lower level
### Chōsui no kamae 澄水之構 - clear water
### Tenchi no kamae 天地之構 - heaven-earth
### Hassō no kamae 八相之構 - eight aspects
### Seigan no kamae 正眼之構 - correct eye
### Kasumi no amae 霞之構 - mist


## Kata 型

### Tsuki komi 附込 - attach and push in
### Tsuki gake 突掛 - thrust and trap
### Kiri age 斬上 - rising cut
### Kiri sage 斬下 – dropping cut
### Kasugaidome 鎹止 – bind and stop
### Kochō gaeshi 小蝶返 – small butterfly counter
### Shihō giri 四方斬 – cutting in four directions
### Happō giri 八方斬 – cutting in eight/all directions
### Tsuki no wa 月之輪 – full moon
