# Kyūsho 急所

The below list is a transliteration of Kyūsho from Koto and Takagi yoshin ryū – as stated previously this will not be a guide to the actual techiques and in this instance is not a guide to the actual Kyūsho, but instead an interesting look at the meaning of the vital points themselves – for the kyusho find a teacher, train, train some more and then you might figure them out.
