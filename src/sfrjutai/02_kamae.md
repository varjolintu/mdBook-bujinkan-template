# Kamae 構

## Za kamae fudoza 坐之構 不動坐 – seated posture, immovable seat
## Hira ichimonji no kamae 平一文字之構 – flat straight line posture
## Seigan no kamae 青眼之構 – aiming at the eye posture
## Katate seigan no kamae 片手青眼之構 – single-hand aiming at the eye posture
## Shizen no kamae 自然之構 – natural posture
